This application is designed for linguists in distinguishing phonemes whose distinctions are not relevant in General American English. This is helpful when exposed to different languages and dialects that make different distinctions.

At the moment, this application only has vowels. In the future, consonants will be added as well.


Here's how it works:

Listen to the sound that is played. Then choopse which one of the options you think was played. An IPA vowel chart is provided.
